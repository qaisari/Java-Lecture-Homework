About Opening Positions

This form opens a market order on the OANDA trading platform for the selected currency pair.

  • Units: Number of base currency units to trade
  • Positive units: Buy (long) position
  • Negative units: Sell (short) position
  • Example: 1000 units = buy 1000 EUR in EUR/USD
Warning: Trading involves risk. This is a real trading account.
