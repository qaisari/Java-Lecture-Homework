About Closing Positions

When you close a position, your unrealized profit or loss becomes realized and is added to your account balance.

  • Realized P/L: The actual profit or loss after closing
  • Permanent: Closing cannot be undone
  • Balance Update: Your account balance updates immediately
Tip: Check the current price before closing to ensure you're happy with the realized P/L.
