About Historical Data

This form retrieves historical candlestick data from OANDA. Candlesticks show open, high, low, and close (OHLC) prices for each time period.

  • M1: 1-minute candles
  • H1: 1-hour candles
  • D: Daily candles
  • W: Weekly candles
  • M: Monthly candles
